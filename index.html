Spelling guide

“Parfumes” is often used as a variant spelling of perfumes (plural) or parfum. In practice, the shopping intent is the same: people want fragrance recommendations or want to understand concentration labels.

  • Perfume: common English term for a fragrance product.
  • Fragrance: broader, neutral term for men’s/women’s/unisex scents.
  • Parfum / Parfume: common in EU usage; can also mean a concentration label (“Parfum”).
